A&O College and Career - Ages 18 to 30

Thursday 7 PM

A&O College and Career provides an encouraging, relaxed environment for 18-30 year olds to come together, drink coffee, nibble on snacks, support one another, and learn about God's Word and His plan for their lives. There's plenty of fun and fellowship, and you're almost guaranteed to read or discuss something life-changing every week. Whether you're a long-time Christian, or just now exploring issues of faith, A&O is equally effective and enjoyable!

The Underground - High School

Sunday 6-8 PM

Meeting in SeaCoast's youth facility, 'The Cellar', The Underground is meant to solidify high schoolers' relationship with Christ. While there is plenty of fun in the form of crazy games, fun events, and once-in-a-lifetime trips, members of The Underground can expect to be personally challenged to live out their faith, examine the scriptures and discover the true meaning of 'worship'. We don't shy away from tough questions. Our format switches between units of Small Group discussion and study for several weeks to units of messages for several weeks. The Underground is open to all 9th - 12th graders.

Area 51 - Junior High

Friday 7-9 PM

Area 51 gets its name from 1 Peter 2:11 (NIV) and is home to our middle school youth group. Meeting every Friday from 7-9pm, Area 51 engages youth with challenging and fun messages aimed at what they're experiencing on a daily basis. Throw in raucous games, fun trips and events, energetic worship and a ton of surprises and you have a 'can't miss' experience for 6th - 8th graders.

Sunday School

Sunday 11 AM

Each Sunday morning, children take part in a hands-on experience in 'Splash Zone', our aquatic-themed children's area, as they learn about the Bible. Sunday school classes are available during the 11:00am service for children 4 years old- 5th grade. A nursery is also available for babies & toddlers.
