Classic Service

Sunday at 9:15 AM

You'll be greeted by a friendly group of people. Traditional hymns are led by a worship leader with organ and piano accompaniment. The same challenging and encouraging biblical messages are presented at both services. A typical service lasts approximately 70 minutes.

Contemporary Service

Sunday at 11 AM

Expect to be greeted by friendly people of all ages. You'll enjoy live worship band accompaniment and modern praise songs. Get ready to be presented with a challenging and encouraging message from the Bible with a few creative twists along the way. A typical service lasts approximately 70 minutes.
