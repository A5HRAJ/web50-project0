Andy Steiger's The Thinking Series - Ages 18 to 30

Thursday 7 PM

In this six-session study, We will openly discuss and explore answers to tough questions such as:

  • “What is the meaning of life?”
  • “Does God exist?”
  • “Do all religions lead to the same God?”
  • “Why is there evil?”
  • “Is there life after death?”

Perfect for the non-Christian and long-time Christian alike, this study will deepen your faith and help you understand God's nature and our relationship with Him.

Dave Ramsey's Financial Peace University

Sunday 6-8 PM

Through video teaching, class discussions and interactive small group activities, FPU presents Biblical, practical steps to get from where you are to where you've dreamed you could be! This plan will show you how to get rid of your debt, manage your money, spend and save wisely and much more!

Guy Waters' Life and Theology of Paul

Thursday at 1 PM

Before being beaten and imprisoned, planting multiple churches, and authoring much of the New Testament, Paul had no equal as a Pharisee in his dedication to the law and his persecution of the early church-but God called Paul to be an Apostle. Join Dr. Guy Waters in this new 12-part series as he identifies key moments in Paul's life and considers the essence of Paul's God-centered theology. Paul's inspired writings and the story of his life continue to be a precious gift to the church, used by God to encourage and equip His people in every generation.

David Barton's Foundations of Freedom

Saturday at 10 AM

Join David Barton and many special guests as they explore the America our Founding Fathers envisioned. From the formation of American law and the judicial system to Biblically-based economics, science, government and more- discover how our Founding Fathers used the Bible as a blueprint for America's freedoms and build your faith in our nation's future.
